About Code Craft Academy

Welcome to Code Craft Academy, where we transform coding enthusiasts into professional developers. Founded in 2018, we've helped over 50,000 students worldwide launch their tech careers.

Our mission is simple: make high-quality coding education accessible to everyone. We believe that anyone can learn to code with the right guidance, resources, and community support.

What sets us apart is our hands-on approach to learning. We don't just teach theory - we guide you through building real projects that you can add to your portfolio and show to potential employers.

Our Teaching Philosophy

  • Learn by Doing: 80% of your time will be spent coding, not watching
  • Project-Based: Every course includes multiple real-world projects
  • Community-First: Learn with peers, not in isolation
  • Career-Focused: Skills that employers actually want
  • Lifetime Access: Learn at your own pace, forever
