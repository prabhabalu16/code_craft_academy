About Code Crafts Academy

Our Mission

At Code Crafts Academy, our mission is to democratize coding education and empower individuals with the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy. We believe that anyone, regardless of background, can learn to code and build amazing things.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of software engineers and educators, Code Crafts Academy started as a small community initiative to teach coding to underprivileged youth. Today, we've grown into a premier online learning platform serving thousands of students worldwide.
